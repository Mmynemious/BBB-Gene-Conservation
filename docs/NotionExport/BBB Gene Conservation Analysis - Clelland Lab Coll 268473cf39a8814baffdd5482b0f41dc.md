# BBB Gene Conservation Analysis - Clelland Lab Collaboration

**Research Question:** How similar are the genomes of human, non-human primate (rhesus macaque) and mouse across blood-brain barrier (BBB) genes?

---

## Project Overview

**Objective:** Compare BBB gene conservation across species to understand evolutionary relationships and validate animal models for neurological disease research.

**Species Analysis:**

- Human (*Homo sapiens*)
- Rhesus macaque (*Macaca mulatta*)
- Mouse (*Mus musculus*)

**Comparison Regions:**

- Coding regions (exons)
- Non-coding regions (introns, UTRs)
- Regulatory regions (promoters, enhancers)
- All splice isoforms included
